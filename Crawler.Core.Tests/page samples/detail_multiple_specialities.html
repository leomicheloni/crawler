    Professional Statement

    Dr. Amal Al Qedra is a UAE national and a OB/GYN specialist who owns her new clinic in Sharjah. She graduated in 1991 and is working as a Senior Specialist OB/GYN Department with the Dubai Health Authority as well as at her own clinic. Dr. Amal holds an Arab Board Specialty in OB/GYN and also holds a Diploma in Gynaecological Endoscopy from India in 2010. 

    Dr. Al Qedra is well versed with early detection of cervical and endometrial cancer and performing surgical procedures for gynaecological cases including Laparoscopy and Hysteroscopy. Dr. Al Qedra strives to use the latest methods for screening and investigations.

    Education

    • Diploma in Laparoscopic Surgeries, August 2010, New Delhi, India .
